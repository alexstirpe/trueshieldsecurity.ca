About True Shield Security

Mission

Our mission is to make security accessible, affordable, and stress-free. We earn your trust one client at a time, as we demonstrate our committment while prioritizing the well-being of our team, knowing that their hard work and dedication is the pillar of our service.

Vision

To become one of the leading providers of security services in Canada, known for our customer service, quick response, professionalism, open flexibilty, affordable rates and dedication to client requirements.

Meet the Owners:

Anwar Bonimy

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

As the Founder and President of True Shield Security Services, Anwar oversees all business operations, strategic growth initiatives, and client partnerships. With over 25 years of experience in the security industry, Anwar brings deep expertise in operational management, business development, and leading large-scale security teams.

Balwinder Bhatia

President & Chief Operations Officer
